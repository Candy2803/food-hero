Food Heroes

Food Heroes was launched on July 12, 2025, during a local hackathon where a team of passionate problem-solvers came together with one goal: to stop food waste and feed more people. What began as a 24-hour challenge quickly turned into a real-world mission. With a simple idea and a powerful platform, Food Heroes connects surplus food from homes, restaurants, and events to those who need it most. Built in a day—made to make a difference every day.

The Daily Waste We Can’t Afford

Every day, restaurants around the world throw away tons of perfectly edible food. In fact, it's estimated that the food service industry wastes up to 11.4 million tons of food each year—translating to thousands of tons every single day. From unsold meals and overstocked ingredients to plate waste, much of this food ends up in landfills, contributing to both environmental harm and missed opportunities to feed those in need. Reducing this waste isn’t just about saving food—it’s about creating a more sustainable and compassionate system for everyone.

Every plate of food that goes uneaten in a restaurant represents more than just lost money—it’s lost resources, energy, and the chance to feed someone in need. Across the globe, restaurants waste an enormous amount of food each day, often due to over-preparation, oversized portions, or strict food safety regulations. While some waste is unavoidable, much of it is perfectly edible and could be redirected to people instead of trash bins. Addressing this daily waste isn’t just a business issue—it’s a social and environmental responsibility.

Why Us?

At Food Heroes, we don’t just talk about change—we make it happen. Our platform bridges the gap between surplus food and hungry people, offering a simple, reliable way to reduce waste and support communities. Whether you're a restaurant with extra meals or someone looking to make a difference, we make giving back easy, safe, and impactful. With real-time tracking, local partnerships, and a heart-driven mission, we turn everyday leftovers into fresh opportunities to fight hunger. Join us—because every meal matters.
